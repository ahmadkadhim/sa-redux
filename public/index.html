    • Philosophy

    • Be Scientific

      Anything we do at Sense Appeal, from baking our pastries to roasting our coffee has been researched extensively. We ground ourselves in peer-reviewed articles in scientific journals, understanding the molecular chemistry that goes on to create what we serve. Based on this research, we conduct our own experiments to see what we can discover.

    • Be Sustainable

      There’s no way we can live separately from the environment. We need to work alongside nature so that we can ensure nutritious food is available not just to our generations but those to come. That means being as nice to the planet as we can. Working with sustainable farms to source our produce, meats and coffees while being as green as we can be.

    • Be In Motion

      Stagnation is death. To be constantly creating, you need to be in motion. What we do today might be proven less efficient tomorrow. We’re always open to your thoughts, comments and suggestions. We will always be redesigning. We will always be in search of new ideas, cuisines and techniques.
